What Is Climate Change — And Why It Matters

Discover the science behind global warming and the key factors driving environmental change.

What Is Climate Change?

Climate change describes long-term shifts in global temperatures and weather patterns. While Earth’s climate has always changed naturally, human activities—like burning fossil fuels, deforestation, and industrial pollution—have dramatically accelerated these changes.

Human Impact

Activities such as fossil fuel consumption and deforestation increase greenhouse gases.

Global Effects

Rising temperatures and unpredictable weather threaten ecosystems, agriculture, and communities.

Urgency

Immediate action is needed to slow the pace of change and protect future generations.
