Why Climate Change Matters

Climate change is not a distant threat — it is happening now. Rising global temperatures, extreme weather events, melting ice caps, and rising sea levels are already affecting communities around the world.

Rising Temperatures

Global temperatures continue to increase, leading to heatwaves and ecosystem disruption.

Sea Level Rise

Melting glaciers and polar ice are causing sea levels to rise, threatening coastal cities.

Extreme Weather

Storms, floods, and wildfires are becoming more frequent and intense worldwide.
